Project: Rider - Endless Runner
Course: CSE (Computer Science & Engineering)
Duration: One month (September 2017 - October 2017)
Type of Project: Partner

The objective of our project was to make a game using Scratch. My role in the project was to develop the game and fix bugs alongside my partner. I was in charge of the formatting of the screens and other aspects of the game. I learned to number your versions of the game and how to effectively communicate with groups members since this was something I was lacking and was crucial for the project. Some challenges I overcame were procrastination and communication errors. Some teamwork skills I used were effective through communication, through e-mail or talking in person, dividing the workload to suit each others needs, and to work with someone effectively. This game uses logic-based code to make it more challenging. To play click the green flag, start, and then once more anywhere in the game, use up and down keys to dodge fireballs. Click the green flag to play again.
